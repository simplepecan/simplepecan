Pumpkin pie... No thank you...
November 26, 2015

Some love pumpkin pie for the holidays, but I think this is because they have not tried the rustic perfection of Pecan Pie. Nothing beats the warm flavor of pecan complemented by the crunch. Pumpkin Pie is a great option if you are short on teeth, but nothing beats the sensory experience of biting into a mouthwatering slice of Pecan Pie with the perfect blend of textures--smooth and crunchy. For me, you can pass the Pecan Pie and keep the Pumpkin. Happy Holidays!

Hamburgers are hum-drum
December 1, 2015

A great hamburger will always have a special place in my heart and stomach on the 4th of July--'Merika! However, I can not do hamburgers any more when I go out. There is just nothing special going on there anymore. You can make it with Kobe beef, put an egg on it, throw an avocado slice on it, or even load it down with pineapple, but at the end of the day you are just complicating a classic. It is a good classic, so don't mess with it. I think it is time to establish the Meatloaf Sandwich as a competitor to the hamburger. There are endless recipes and lots of room for creative genius using subtle ingredients, instead of throwing leftovers on a sandwich. So, put down that hum-drum hambuger and give meatloaf a chance.

The Art of the Morning Ritual
December 5, 2015

Mastering my morning ritual is essential for my well-being. I hate the brief times when I just moved somewhere new and struggle to find the right ritual. For me, it starts with finding right the place for a light breakfast and a nice pour of dark roast coffee. I find my focus for the day as I slowly nibble on a delicious pastry and sip my coffee while catching up on social media and inking my fingers on the latest copies of the New York Times and Wall Street Journal. When I do not have the chance to enjoy this part of my day, I feel something is missing-- like I left the house not sure whether I locked the door. I feel unsettled and can never find clarity in my thoughts as I fight through the rest of the day. Mastering the morning ritual is a necessity for me and I hope you can master yours.
